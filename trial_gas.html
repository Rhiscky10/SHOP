Our Mission

  • Market quality petroleum and other products and services in an ethical, healthy, safe, environmentally friendly, and socially responsible manner.
  • Produce and manufacture goods and services that enhance the marketing, distribution, and sale of the company's products and services.


    • CORE VALUES

  • The core values of the company are represented with the acronym, G-O-I-L.

    G stands for Good

    O stands for Originality

    I for Integrity

    L Represents Leadership

  • The company thus prides itself with good and quality products and services it provides; its originality and commitment to being a Ghanaian brand; credibility to maintaining integrity; as well as growth and years of industry leadership.


    • OUR VISION

    To be a world-class provider of goods and services in the petroleum and other areas of the energy industry.
